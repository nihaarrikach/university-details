AKTU LUCKNOW ADMISSIONS

Admissions at the APJ Abdul Kalam Technical University for UG and PG courses are based on the UPSEE entrance test. The university also accepts JEE Mains scores for B.Tech admissions. Candidates applying for M.Tech are eligible for university counseling through GATE scores. For admission to AKTU PhD programs, the institution conducts a separate research-based entrance test followed by a personal interview round for the shortlisted candidates.
